BCBS VT App

A SwiftUI companion app for Blue Cross Blue Shield of Vermont — built to solve problems the official digital experience doesn't.

Why This Exists

I've been frustrated for years by the lack of digital insurance cards for Apple Wallet. A few companies offer them, but the implementations are consistently poor — ugly design, missing information, no real integration with the rest of the phone. My own insurer, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Vermont, doesn't offer one at all.

So I decided to build what they wouldn't. Not a replacement for their entire platform, but a focused companion app that handles the things their digital experience should do — without the constraints of internal systems, vendor relationships, and legacy infrastructure that hold back most insurance technology.

What It Does

  • Latest news pulled directly from BCBSVT
  • Receipt scanning and tracking using on-device OCR
  • Digital member ID cards with plan details and deductible tracking
  • AI chat assistant powered by Apple Intelligence for benefits questions

How It's Built

Built in SwiftUI with Claude as a coding partner. CloudKit backend (free at this scale, zero server management). AI chat uses on-device models so health questions never leave your phone. Provider directory pulls from the same live database BCBSVT uses.

This is a design and implementation playground — not something I plan on releasing publicly. But it demonstrates what one person with a set of API keys and a clear problem can build.
