The Sunshine Trail

An interactive map for Lawson's Finest Liquids showing Sunshine Spots from Waitsfield, Vermont to Asheville, North Carolina.

The Story

Lawson's Finest Liquids wanted to show the communities their beer reaches â€” not just where you can buy it, but the retailers, hiking trails, river put-ins, and community organizations they support along the way. The Sunshine Trail maps that journey from their home in Vermont down the East Coast.

Impact

The map includes live impact metrics: $3 million raised, over 2 million kWh of solar generated, a B Impact Score of 83.6, and support for 496 nonprofits. These numbers update from real data.

How It's Built

Interactive map with custom markers, clustering, and a sidebar showing community impact. Built to tell a brand story through geography.
